Welcome, I'm Huda

Hello! I'm Huda Hendricks, also known as Lunar — your dedicated lash and brow technician, here to enhance your natural beauty with precision and care.

With a passion for creating stunning lash extensions and perfectly shaped brows, I strive to provide a relaxing, luxurious experience that leaves you feeling confident and pampered. I love meeting new faces and catching up with familiar ones — every appointment is personal to me.

My journey began with a deep love for beauty and a commitment to mastering the art of lash and brow enhancement. Born and raised in Cape Town, I've spent over 6 years growing within this industry and refining my craft. I specialise in tailoring each treatment to suit your unique features, ensuring a personalised and flawless result every time.

Visit me in Pinelands, where professionalism meets a warm, welcoming atmosphere. Let's enhance your natural beauty together.

Appointment Policy

All appointments are booked online through our scheduling system, Fresha. Once confirmed, you will receive a confirmation email and a reminder 24 hours prior.

Alternatively, feel free to message on WhatsApp to make a booking.

Payments Policy

Payments must be made prior to the appointment or before leaving. Please send proof of payment to our email or WhatsApp.

We accept Cash or EFT. Card facilities are coming soon. Gratuities are not included but always appreciated.

Cancellations

Rescheduling or cancellations must be made 24 hours prior to your appointment.

If not made within this period, you are liable to pay 50% of your treatment fee in order to book with us again.

Late Arrival

Please be punctual. If you are late, we will try to help, but your time cannot be extended.

Arrivals over 20 minutes late will be automatically cancelled. No-shows are liable for 50% of the treatment fee.
